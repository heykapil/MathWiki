``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\phi:M\to N$ be an $R$-module homomorphism. The <b>kernel of $\phi$</b> is the submodule $\ker\phi\coloneqq\phi^{-1}\!\l(0\r)$ of $M$.
* Dually, the <b>cokernel of $\phi$</b> is the quotient module $\coker\phi\coloneqq N/\im\phi$.

```
