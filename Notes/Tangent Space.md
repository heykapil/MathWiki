``` ad-Definition
title: Definition (Tangent Space).

Let $M$ be a smooth manifold and fix $p\in M$. The <b>tangent space</b> of $M$ at $p$ is the vector space $T_pM\coloneqq\Der_\R\!\l(C^\infty\!\l(M\r),\R\r)$.

```
