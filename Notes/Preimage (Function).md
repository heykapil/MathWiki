``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $f:X\to Y$ and fix $T\subseteq Y$. The <b>preimage of $T$ under $f$</b> is the set
$$\begin{equation}
    \preim_f\l(T\r)\coloneqq\l\{x\in X\st f\l(x\r)\in T\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
