``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $Y\overset{f}{\rightarrow}X\overset{p}{\leftarrow}E$ be continuous maps between topological spaces. A map $\widetilde{f}:Y\to E$ is said to be a <b>lifting of $f$ w.r.t. $p$</b> if $p\circ\widetilde{f}=f$.
```
