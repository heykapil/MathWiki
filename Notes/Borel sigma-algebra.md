``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $X$ be a topological space. The <b>Borel $\sigma$-algebra</b> on $X$ is the $\sigma$-algebra generated by the topology of $X$, denoted $\mc{B}\l(X\r)$.

```

<b>Remark.</b> Elements in $\mc{B}\l(X\r)$ are called <i>Borel sets</i>.
