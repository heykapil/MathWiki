``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $R$ be a ring. A left/right/two-sided ideal $\mf{a}\nsubgrp R$ is said to be <b>principal</b> if $\mf{a}=Ra$ (resp. $\mf{a}=aR$, $\mf{a}=\gen{a}$) for some $a\in R$, in which case $a$ is said to be a <b>generator for $\mf{a}$</b>.

```
