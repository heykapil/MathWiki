``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,\leq}$ be a poset and fix $S\subseteq X$. The <b>least element of $S$</b> is an element $l\in S$ such that $\fa s\in S:l\leq s$. Similarly for the <b>greatest element</b>.

```
