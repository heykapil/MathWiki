``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $M$ and $N$ be left $R$-modules. An <b>$R$-module homomorphism from $M$ to $N$</b> is a function $\phi:M\to N$ such that $\phi\l(rm+n\r)=r\phi\l(m\r)+\phi\l(n\r)$ for all $r\in R$ and $m,n\in M$.

```
