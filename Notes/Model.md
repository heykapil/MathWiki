``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $T$ be a $\sigma$-theory. A non-empty $\sigma$-structure $\mc{A}$ is said to <b>model</b> $T$, written $\mc{A}\models T$, if $\mc{A}\models\phi$ for every $\phi\in T$.

```
