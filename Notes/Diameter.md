``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,d}$ be a metric space. The <b>diameter of $X$</b> is $\diam X\coloneqq\sup_{x,y\in X}d\l(x,y\r)$, where we take $\diam X\coloneqq\infty$ if the supremum does not exist.

```
