title: Definition (Euler’s Number).

The sequence $\tpl{\l(1+\frac{1}{n}\r)^n}_{n\in\N^+}$ in $\R$ converges to <b>Euler’s Number</b>
$$\begin{equation}
    e\coloneqq\lim\limits_{n\to\infty}\l(1+\frac{1}{n}\r)^n.
\end{equation}$$
