``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $X$ be a set and let $R$ be a binary relation on $X$. The <b>reflexive closure of $R$ w.r.t. $X$</b> is the binary relation
$$\begin{equation}
    \rfcl R\coloneqq R\cup\id_X.
\end{equation}$$

```
