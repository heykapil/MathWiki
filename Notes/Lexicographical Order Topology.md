title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,<_X}$ and $\tpl{Y,<_Y}$ be totally ordered sets and consider the lexicographical order $<$ on $X\times Y$. The <b>lexicographical order topology on $X\times Y$</b> is the order topology $\mc{T}(<)$.
