``` ad-Theorem
title: Theorem (Invariant Basis Number).

Let $M$ be an $R$-module over a commutative ring $R$. If $X_1$ and $X_2$ are bases for $M$, then $\l|X_1\r|=\l|X_2\r|$.

```
