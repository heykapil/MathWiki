``` ad-Theorem
title: Theorem (Carathéodory’s Extension Theorem).

Let $\tpl{X,\mc{A}}$ be a measurable set. Every $\sigma$-finite premeasure $\mu$ on $\mc{A}$ admits a unique extension to a measure on $\gen{\mc{A}}_\sigma$.
```
