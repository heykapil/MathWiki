``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $V$ be a $K$-vector space and consider a linear subspace $U\subseteq V$. A <b>complement of $U$ in $V$</b> is a linear subspace $W\subseteq V$ such that $V=U\oplus W$.

```
