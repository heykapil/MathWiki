``` ad-Theorem
title: Theorem (Linear Invertibility Theorem).

Let $\alpha\in\End\l(M\r)$ be a linear operator in a fin-gen. free $R$-module $M$. Then $\alpha\in\GL\l(M\r)$ iff $\det\alpha\in R^\times$.

```
