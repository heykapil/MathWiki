``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,\leq}$ be a poset and fix $S\subseteq X$. A <b>minimal element of $S$</b> is an element $m\in S$ such that $\fa s\in S:\lnot\l(s<m\r)$. Similarly for <b>maximal elements</b>.

```
