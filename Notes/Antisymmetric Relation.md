title: Definition.

Let $X$ be a set. A binary relation $R$ is <b>antisymmetric on $X$</b> if
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa x,y\in X:\l(xRy\land yRx\r)\Rightarrow x=y.
\end{equation}$$
