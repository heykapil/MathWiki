``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

A group $G$ is said to be <b>solvable</b> if it admits a (finite) abelian series $G=G_0\nsupgrp G_1\nsupgrp\cdots\nsupgrp G_n=\l\{e\r\}$.

```
