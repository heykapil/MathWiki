``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{a_k}$ be a sequence in $\R$ or $\C$ and fix a bijection $\sigma:\N\to\N$. The <b>$\sigma$-rearrangement of $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty}a_k$</b> is the infinite series $\sum_{k=0}^{\infty}a_{\sigma\l(k\r)}$.

```
