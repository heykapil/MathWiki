``` ad-Proposition
title: Proposition.

Let $\l\{X_i\r\}_{i\in I}$ be an indexed family of sets. Then $\prod_{i\in I}X_i$, together with the projection maps $\pi_j:\prod_{i\in I}X_i\to X_j$, is a product in $\catset$.

```
