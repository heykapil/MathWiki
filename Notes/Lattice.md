``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

A poset $\tpl{X,\leq}$ is called a <b>meet-semilattice</b> (resp. <b>join-semilattice</b>) if every pair $\l\{x,y\r\}$ admits an infimum (resp. supremum).
* A <b>lattice</b> is a poset that is both a meet and a join semilattice.

```
