``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $x$ and $y$ be sets. An <b>ordered pair of $x$ and $y$</b> is the set $\tpl{x,y}\coloneqq\l\{\l\{x\r\},\l\{x,y\r\}\r\}$.
```
