``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $R$ be a binary relation. The <b>inverse of $R$</b> is the relation
$$\begin{equation}
    R^{-1}\coloneqq\l\{\tpl{y,x}\st xRy\r\}.
\end{equation}$$

```

<b>Remark.</b> Observe that $xRy\Leftrightarrow yR^{-1}x$.<span style="float:right;">$\blacklozenge$</span>
