``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,\leq}$ be a poset and fix $S\subseteq X$. A <b>lower bound of $S$ in $X$</b> is an element $l\in X$ such that $\fa s\in S:l\leq s$. Similarly for <b>upper bounds</b>.

```
