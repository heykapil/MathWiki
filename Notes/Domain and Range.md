``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $R$ be a binary relation. The <b>domain and range of $R$</b> are the sets
$$\begin{equation}
    \dom R\coloneqq\l\{x\st\ex y:xRy\r\}\ \ \ \ \mathit{and}\ \ \ \ \ran R\coloneqq\l\{y\st\ex x:xRy\r\},
\end{equation}$$
respectively.
```
