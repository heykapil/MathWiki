title: Definition.

Let $\tpl{X,<}$ be a poset. A subset $Y\subseteq X$ is called an <b>initial segment of $X$ w.r.t. $<$</b> if
$$\begin{equation}
    \fa y\in Y,\fa x\in X:x<y\Rightarrow x\in Y.
\end{equation}$$
