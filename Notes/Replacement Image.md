``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $D$ be a set and let $\phi\l(x,y\r)$ be a formula such that $\fa x\in D,\ex!y:\phi\l(x,y\r)$. The <b>replacement image of $D$ under $\phi\l(x,y\r)$</b> is the set
$$\begin{equation}
    \im_\phi\!\l(D\r)\coloneqq\l\{y\st\ex x\in D:\phi\l(x,y\r)\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
