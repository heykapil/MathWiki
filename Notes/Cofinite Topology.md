``` ad-Definition
title: Definition.

Let $X$ be a set. The <b>cofinite topology on $X$</b> is the topology
$$\begin{equation}
    \mc{T}_\textrm{cf}\coloneqq\l\{U\in\pow\l(X\r)\st\l(U=\em\r)\lor\l(X\comp U\ \mathit{is}\ \mathit{finite}\r)\r\}.
\end{equation}$$
```
